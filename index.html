About Us

Basthi Ki Pathshala began as a humble initiative in the heart of local communities, driven by the belief that every child deserves access to quality education and nurturing mentorship. What started with a few volunteers and weekend classes has blossomed into a vibrant learning ecosystem.

We work hand-in-hand with families, educators, and changemakers to create safe, inclusive spaces where children can dream big and grow with confidence. Our programs blend academic support with emotional guidance, ensuring no child is left behind.
